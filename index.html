People always talk about one defining moment. For me, there wasn't one. It was a collection of quiet moments that, before I knew it, had become something much bigger than themselves.

The way you get so genuinely excited over the little gestures you and your sister share always makes me smile. It's such a small thing, but it says so much about you. It reminds me how deeply you love the people around you and how much you treasure even the simplest moments with them. There's something incredibly beautiful about someone whose heart finds joy in the ordinary, and can make the ordinary feel extraordinary.

The way your face completely lights up as you reveal a ridiculously massive burger. I genuinely didn't know it was possible to get so much happiness from watching another human bean enjoy their dinner, but somehow you've proven me wrong ㅋㅋㅋㅋ But it's never really been about the burger—it's always been about how happy you look.

Even little moments like this have become strangely precious to me. There's nothing particularly exciting about brushing your teeth, yet somehow I catch myself smiling through the entire thing. This isn't something most people would call romantic, but with you, even the most mundane moments make me wish I were standing right there beside you. I think that's because it's never really been about what we're doing—it's just that I like sharing life with you, even in its most ordinary moments.

By some miracle, I'm lucky enough to be the one who gets to make you laugh like this. Every laugh, every smile, every time I catch that look on your face only makes me want to find another reason to see it again. But what I admire even more is what happens when things don't go perfectly. When my words don't come out the way I intended or we misunderstand each other, you always find a way to tell me how you feel with kindness instead of resentment and you never make me feel like we're on opposite sides.. Somehow, every difficult conversation leaves us understanding each other a little better than before, and I think that's something truly special.

You have this incredible way of remembering the smallest things about me—the passing comments, the random thoughts, the little details I don't even remember telling you. Then, out of nowhere, you'll bring one of them up weeks later, and I'm left wondering how on earth you remembered it. More than anything, it makes me feel seen in a way I didn't know I needed.

But perhaps what I treasure most is something a little harder to describe. I've met plenty of people I could talk to, but very few I could truly think with. I've never been able to have conversations as thoughtful, challenging, and genuinely stimulating as the ones I have with you. We haven't had nearly as many as I'd like, yet every time we do, I walk away feeling like you've awakened a part of me that's been quietly waiting for someone to meet it. Those conversations never leave me satisfied—they only leave me wanting the next one.

None of these moments are particularly extraordinary on their own. They're quiet, ordinary, and easy to overlook. But together, they've become the reason I keep finding myself wanting to call you, text you, hear your voice, and learn just a little more about you. They're the reason I fell for you. And I hope you never underestimate just how much I appreciate every one of them.

I don't know what your answer will be, and I'd be lying if I said that didn't scare me a little. But I'd rather risk hearing the truth than spend the rest of my time wondering what could have been. So, with more courage than confidence, here it is—the thought that's been quietly growing through every page of this letter..
